```sass
ubuntu@ubuntu-VirtualBox:~/demo$ touch ddd.txt

ubuntu@ubuntu-VirtualBox:~/demo$ mkdir tar

ubuntu@ubuntu-VirtualBox:~/demo/tar$ tar xvf demo.tar
```
